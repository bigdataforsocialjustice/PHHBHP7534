Readings

  • Chapter 4 Babbie, E. R. (2016). The practice of social research (Fourteenth edition). Cengage Learning.(Babbie 2016)
  • Barboza-Salerno, G. E. (2020). Variability and stability in child maltreatment risk across time and space and its association with neighborhood social & housing vulnerability in New Mexico: A bayesian space-time model. Child Abuse & Neglect, 104, 104472. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chiabu.2020.104472 (Barboza-Salerno 2020)
  • Yang, T.-C., Kim, S., Zhao, Y., & Choi, S. E. (2021). Examining spatial inequality in COVID-19 positivity rates across New York City ZIP codes. Health & Place, 69, 102574. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.healthplace.2021.102574 (Yang et al. 2021)
